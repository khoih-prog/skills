# AfrexAI Data Governance Framework

Score your organization's data governance across 6 domains with 48 controls. Get a remediation roadmap prioritized by regulatory risk.

## Domains Covered
- Data Quality (profiling, SLAs, stewardship)
- Data Cataloging (discovery, lineage, glossary)
- Access Control (RBAC, classification, anomaly detection)
- Compliance Mapping (GDPR, CCPA, SOX, HIPAA, PCI-DSS)
- Retention & Lifecycle (archival, legal hold, minimization)
- AI/Agent Data Governance (provenance, PII, explainability)

## Scoring
0-100% across each domain. Overall score = average. Remediation prioritized by risk.
