# Customer Success Playbook

Complete framework for building and scaling a B2B SaaS customer success operation.

Covers onboarding, health scoring, QBRs, churn prevention, expansion revenue, and CSM capacity planning.

- Health score model (7 weighted signals)
- Onboarding checklist with TTFV benchmarks
- QBR template and prep checklist
- 3-tier churn intervention playbook
- Expansion signal detection and plays
- CSM capacity model by segment
- Metrics dashboard specification

- CS leaders redesigning their playbook
- Revenue ops teams optimizing NRR
- Anyone targeting net revenue retention above 110%
