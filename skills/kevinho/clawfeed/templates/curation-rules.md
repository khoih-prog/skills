# Twitter Curation Rules

> Customize these rules to control how your AI digest filters and prioritizes content.

## Follow Recommendations

### Must Meet (AND)
1. **Not followed** — Not in current Following list
2. **Active** — Posted within last 30 days
3. **Relevant** — Content matches your configured topics

### Meet At Least One (OR)
- Follower ≥ 10K
- Employee/founder at notable companies in your focus areas
- Recommended/interacted by multiple accounts you follow
- High-quality original content (deep analysis, unique insights)

### Bonus
- Building in public
- Sharing practical experience
- Active builder in your niche

### Exclude
- ❌ Repost-only accounts
- ❌ Primarily memes/jokes
- ❌ Marketing-focused (selling courses, promotions)
- ❌ Primarily controversial political content

---

## Unfollow Suggestions

### Strong Unfollow (any one)
- Inactive > 6 months
- Content shifted to pure marketing/politics
- Spam — 10+ low-quality posts daily
- Left your focus area

### Suggested Unfollow (2+ items)
- Inactive 3-6 months
- Quality declined noticeably
- Mostly reposts, few originals
- No longer relevant to your interests
- Low followers (<1K) with mediocre content

### Never Unfollow (Whitelist)
- Your own accounts
- Industry leaders (even if infrequent, quality when they post)
- Personal connections
- Project official accounts you actively follow

---

## Mark System (Content Deep Dive)

### What is Mark
When you're interested in a specific article/tweet, mark it for a **one-time deep reading analysis**.

### Deep Dive Output
1. **Full translation/summary** (if non-native language)
2. **Core insights** — What the author is saying and why it matters
3. **Analysis** — Background context, connections to your interests
4. **Further reading** — Related discussions, counterpoints, references
5. **One-line takeaway**

---

## Audit Strategy

1. **Sample size**: 20-30 followed accounts per check
2. **Method**: Random + targeted (prioritize accounts absent from feed)
3. **Output**: List only "suggest unfollow" with specific reasons
