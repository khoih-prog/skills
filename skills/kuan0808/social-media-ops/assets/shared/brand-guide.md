# Brand Guide — Universal Principles

These apply across all registered brands (see `brand-registry.md`). Brand-specific details are in `shared/brands/{brand_id}/`.

## Cross-Brand Rules
- Never disparage competitors by name
- All claims must be factually verifiable
- No misleading before/after comparisons
- Respect platform terms of service
- All content requires owner approval before publishing

## Content Language
- Default content language per INSTANCE.md; per-brand override in brand profile.md
- Owner communication language: per INSTANCE.md
- Internal files: English

## Emoji Policy
Use sparingly unless brand guidelines specify otherwise.
