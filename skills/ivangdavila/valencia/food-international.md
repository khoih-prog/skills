# International & Fine Dining in Valencia

## Fine Dining Scene

Valencia punches above its weight in fine dining. Several Michelin-starred restaurants at lower prices than Madrid or Barcelona.

### Michelin-Starred Restaurants

| Restaurant | Stars | Chef | Price Range | Style |
|------------|-------|------|-------------|-------|
| Ricard Camarena | 2★ | Ricard Camarena | €180-220 | Modern Valencian |
| El Poblet | 1★ | Quique Dacosta team | €100-150 | Contemporary |
| Fierro | 1★ | Camarena group | €80-100 | Casual fine dining |

### Ricard Camarena Restaurant
- **Location**: Near Bioparc
- **Style**: Modern Valencian with global techniques
- **Experience**: Full tasting menu, 3-4 hours
- **Book**: 3-4 weeks ahead
- **Price**: €180-220 tasting menu

### El Poblet
- **Location**: Centro
- **Style**: Quique Dacosta's city outpost
- **Experience**: More accessible than his Dénia flagship
- **Book**: 2-3 weeks ahead
- **Price**: €100-150

### Fierro
- **Location**: Ruzafa area
- **Style**: Casual gastronomy
- **Experience**: Shorter menu, relaxed vibe
- **Book**: 1 week ahead
- **Price**: €80-100

## Brunch Scene

Valencia has embraced brunch culture, especially in Ruzafa.

### Best Brunch Spots

| Restaurant | Location | Price | Style |
|------------|----------|-------|-------|
| Federal | Ruzafa | €15-25 | Australian, crowded |
| Bluebell Coffee | Ruzafa | €12-20 | Best coffee, light food |
| Copenhagen | Ruzafa | €15-25 | Scandinavian, healthy |
| La Fábrica | Ruzafa | €12-20 | Industrial, popular |
| Dulce de Leche | Ruzafa | €10-18 | Argentine pastries |
| Ubik Café | Ruzafa | €10-15 | Books + breakfast |

### Brunch Tips
- **Weekend reservations**: Book Friday-Sunday
- **Peak hours**: 11:00-13:00 packed
- **Go early**: 10:00 for seats without wait
- **Coffee quality**: Bluebell, Federal have best beans

| Restaurant | Location | Price | Type |
|------------|----------|-------|------|

| Almalibre | Ruzafa | €10-15 | All vegan |
| La Lluna | Centro | €12-18 | Vegetarian Spanish |
| Ana Eva | Centro | €12-18 | Vegetarian |

### Vegan Tips
- **Ruzafa**: Best selection by far
- **Traditional restaurants**: Ask for "platos sin carne ni pescado"
- **Watch for**: Jamón in everything, fish stock in rice
- **Apps**: HappyCow essential
