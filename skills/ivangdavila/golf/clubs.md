## Club Selection Tips

### Wind Adjustments
| Wind | Adjustment |
|------|------------|
| 10 mph into | +1 club |
| 10 mph helping | -1 club |
| 20 mph into | +2 clubs |
| Crosswind | Aim for center, let drift |

### Elevation
- **Uphill:** +1 club per 10 yards elevation
- **Downhill:** -1 club per 10 yards drop

### Temperature
- **Cold (<50F):** Ball flies 5-10% shorter
- **Hot (>90F):** Ball flies slightly longer

### Lie Adjustments
| Lie | Adjustment |
|-----|------------|
| Fairway | Standard |
| Light rough | Ball may fly, less spin |
| Heavy rough | Shorter, use more loft |
| Uphill lie | Aim right, ball draws |
| Downhill lie | Aim left, ball fades |
| Ball above feet | Aim right |
| Ball below feet | Aim left |

## Fitting Basics

### Driver
- **Slice:** Consider draw bias, closed face
- **Hook:** Neutral or fade bias
- **Low launch:** More loft, lighter shaft
- **High launch:** Less loft, stiffer shaft

### Irons
| Swing Speed | Shaft Flex |
|-------------|------------|
| <75 mph | Senior/Ladies |
| 75-85 mph | Regular |
| 85-95 mph | Stiff |
| 95+ mph | X-Stiff |

### Putter
- **Arc stroke:** Toe hang putter
- **Straight stroke:** Face-balanced putter
