# Food Guide — Los Angeles

## What Makes LA Food Special

LA is one of America's best food cities. Key strengths:

1. **Best Mexican food in America** — Not just burritos (SF-style doesn't exist here), but authentic regional Mexican
2. **Korean food capital** — Koreatown is the largest Korean community outside Korea
3. **Japanese food excellence** — Sushi rivals Tokyo, plus ramen, izakaya
4. **Global diversity** — Armenian, Thai, Ethiopian, Filipino, Persian, Vietnamese
5. **Taco culture** — Street tacos are religion, 24/7 availability
6. **Health/wellness** — Organic everything, smoothie bowls, plant-based pioneers

## Essential LA Food Experiences

### Street Tacos (Non-Negotiable)

You haven't experienced LA until you've had proper street tacos.

| Style | Where | What to Order |
|-------|-------|---------------|
| **Birria tacos** | Birrieria Gonzalez, Teddy's Red Tacos | Quesabirria with consommé |
| **Al Pastor** | Leo's Taco Truck (multiple), Tacos 1986 | Al pastor with piña |
| **Carnitas** | Carnitas el Momo, Sonoratown | Tacos de carnitas |
| **Mariscos** | Mariscos Jalisco, Holbox | Ceviche, shrimp tacos |
| **Tijuana style** | Tacos 1986 | Carne asada |

**Price:** $2-5 per taco

### Korean BBQ (Koreatown Essential)

| Restaurant | Level | Price | Notes |
|------------|-------|-------|-------|
| **Kang Ho-dong Baekjeong** | High-end | $$$$ | Best quality |
| **Park's BBQ** | High-end | $$$$ | Legendary |
| **Quarters Korean BBQ** | Mid-range | $$$ | Great value |
| **Road to Seoul** | AYCE | $$ | Unlimited meat |

**How it works:**
1. Order raw meat
2. Cook at table grill
3. Wrap in lettuce with banchan (sides)
4. Soju is required

## The In-N-Out Reality

You will hear about In-N-Out. Here's the truth:

**What to order:**
- Double-Double (standard, good)
- Animal Style (grilled onions, extra sauce)
- Fries: Animal Style or skip (they're mediocre)

**Reality:** It's good fast food. Not transcendent. Worth trying once. Don't wait 30+ min.

**Secret menu:** Animal style, protein style (lettuce wrap), 4x4, chopped chilies
