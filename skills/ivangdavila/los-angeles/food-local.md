# LA Local Specialties — What to Eat

## Street Tacos (Essential)

**This is the single most important food experience in LA.**

### Styles

| Style | What It Is | Where to Try |
|-------|------------|--------------|
| **Al Pastor** | Pork on vertical spit, pineapple | Leo's Taco Truck (multiple), Tacos 1986 |
| **Birria** | Braised beef/goat, consommé | Birrieria Gonzalez, Teddy's Red Tacos |
| **Carnitas** | Braised pork | Carnitas el Momo, Sonoratown |
| **Carne Asada** | Grilled beef | Tacos 1986, King Taco |
| **Cabeza** | Beef head (tender!) | Any good truck |
| **Lengua** | Tongue (surprisingly good) | Any good truck |
| **Tripas** | Intestine (crispy) | Adventurous eaters |
| **Mariscos** | Seafood | Mariscos Jalisco, Holbox |

### How to Eat
1. Find a truck with a line (good sign)
2. Order 2-3 different tacos
3. Top with onion, cilantro, salsa
4. Squeeze lime
5. Repeat

### Best Taco Spots

| Name | Style | Location |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Leo's Taco Truck** | Al pastor | Multiple (La Brea & Sunset famous) |
| **Tacos 1986** | Tijuana style | DTLA, Hollywood |
| **Sonoratown** | Sonoran | DTLA |
| **Birrieria Gonzalez** | Birria | East LA |
| **Teddy's Red Tacos** | Birria | Compton (worth the drive) |
| **Mariscos Jalisco** | Seafood | East LA (shrimp tacos!) |
| **Guerrilla Tacos** | Elevated | Arts District |

## Korean BBQ (Koreatown Essential)

### How It Works
1. Order raw meat cuts
2. Cook on table grill (server often helps)
3. Wrap in lettuce with rice, banchan (side dishes)
4. Dip in ssamjang sauce
5. Drink soju

### What to Order
| Cut | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Galbi** | Short rib, marinated |
| **Chadolbaegi** | Thin brisket slices |
| **Samgyeopsal** | Pork belly |
| **Bulgogi** | Marinated beef |
| **Dwaeji bulgogi** | Spicy pork |

### Where to Go

| Restaurant | Level | Notes |
|------------|-------|-------|
| **Kang Ho-dong Baekjeong** | Premium | Best quality, celeb chef |
| **Park's BBQ** | Premium | Legendary, dry-aged |
| **Quarters** | Mid-range | Great value |
| **Gen Korean BBQ** | AYCE | Unlimited, decent |
| **Road to Seoul** | AYCE | Budget, late night |

### Tips
- Go with 3+ people (more variety)
- Don't flip meat too much
- Try everything in the banchan
- Soju is essential

## The Breakfast Burrito

LA's other essential handheld food.

### What Makes It LA
- Scrambled eggs
- Potatoes (hash browns or cubed)
- Cheese
- Choice of meat (bacon, chorizo, carne asada)
- Salsa
- Sometimes avocado

### Where to Get One

| Name | Location | Known For |
|------|----------|-----------|
| **Lucky Boy** | Pasadena | Massive size |
| **Tacos Villa Corona** | Atwater | Classic, perfect |
| **Burritos La Palma** | Mid-City | Handmade tortillas |
| **Cofax** | Fairfax | Trendy, excellent |
| **Howlin' Ray's** | Chinatown | Hot chicken burrito |

## In-N-Out Burger

### The Reality
It's good fast food. Not transcendent. Worth trying once.

### How to Order
| Order | What It Is |
|-------|------------|
| **Double-Double** | 2 patties, 2 cheese, standard |
| **Animal Style burger** | Grilled onions, extra sauce |
| **Animal Style fries** | Cheese, sauce, onions |
| **Protein Style** | Lettuce wrap (no bun) |
| **4x4** | 4 patties, 4 cheese |
| **Chopped chilies** | On burger, free |

### Tips
- Fries are mediocre (try Animal Style)
- Don't wait more than 20 min
- Shake is good
- It's fine, not life-changing

## The Erewhon Experience

Erewhon is LA's wellness grocery store. Famous for:
- $25 smoothies
- Supplement section bigger than most pharmacies
- Celebrity sightings
- Overpriced everything

**Try it once** for the experience. Get a smoothie. Accept the $25 price. Move on.
