# East & Northeast Neighborhoods — Los Angeles

Up-and-coming areas east of DTLA. More affordable, artsy, diverse. Gentrification in progress.

---

## Highland Park

### Overview
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Vibe** | Gentrifying, artsy, hipster |
| **1BR Rent** | $1,800-2,500 |
| **Safety** | ★★★☆☆ Improving |
| **Transit** | A Line (Gold) |
| **Parking** | Moderate |

### Who It's For
- Artists on budget
- Those priced out of Silver Lake
- Vintage/thrift shoppers
- Young creatives

### Pros
- Most affordable hipster area
- York Blvd scene (cafes, vintage)
- A Line access
- Growing, improving
- Latino culture + hipster mix

### Cons
- Gentrification tension
- Safety still varies
- Far from Westside
- Some rough blocks
- Getting more expensive

### York Boulevard
Main hip strip with coffee shops, vintage stores, restaurants.
