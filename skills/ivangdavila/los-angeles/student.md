# Student Guide — Los Angeles

## Major Universities

### UCLA (University of California, Los Angeles)

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | Westwood |
| **Type** | Public research university |
| **Undergrad** | ~32,000 |
| **Strengths** | Film, Medicine, Engineering, Business |
| **Campus** | Beautiful, self-contained |

**Living Near UCLA:**
- **Westwood:** Walking distance, expensive ($2,500-3,500 1BR)
- **Palms/Mar Vista:** More affordable, 10-15 min drive
- **Santa Monica:** Bikeable, beach access, expensive

**Budget:** $2,000-2,500/month all-in for modest living

---

### USC (University of Southern California)

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Location** | South LA (near DTLA) |
| **Type** | Private research university |
| **Undergrad** | ~20,000 |
| **Strengths** | Film (top program), Business, Engineering |
| **Campus** | Self-contained, security |

**Living Near USC:**
- **On campus:** Required for many freshmen
- **North of campus:** Improving, still research blocks
- **DTLA:** 10-15 min, more options
- **Koreatown:** Affordable, 15-20 min

**Budget:** $2,200-3,000/month (higher due to private school area)

**Safety Note:** Campus is safe, surrounding area varies significantly.

## Student Budgets

### Monthly Breakdown

| Category | Low | Medium | Comfortable |
|----------|-----|--------|-------------|
| **Housing** | $1,200 (roommates) | $1,800 (studio/share) | $2,500 (1BR) |
| **Food** | $300 | $450 | $600 |
| **Transport** | $100 (bus) | $300 (car share) | $500 (own car) |
| **Phone** | $50 | $80 | $100 |
| **Entertainment** | $100 | $200 | $300 |
| **Books/Supplies** | $50 | $100 | $150 |
| **TOTAL** | **$1,800** | **$2,930** | **$4,150** |

### Saving Money

- **Roommates:** Cut rent by 30-50%
- **Meal prep:** Campus dining is expensive
- **Student discounts:** Metro, museums, software
- **Used books:** Chegg, Amazon, library
- **Free entertainment:** Beaches, hiking, campus events

## Transportation

### Without a Car

| Option | Cost | Reality |
|--------|------|---------|
| **Metro** | $50/month student | Limited but works for some |
| **Bus** | Same | Good in Westside, DTLA |
| **Bike** | One-time | Beach areas, flat areas OK |
| **Uber/Lyft** | Variable | Expensive, emergencies |

### Schools Where No-Car Works
- **UCLA:** Westwood walkable, buses to beach
- **USC:** Metro E Line, DTLA accessible
- **CalTech:** Pasadena walkable, A Line
- **SMC:** Santa Monica very bikeable

### When You Need a Car
- Living far from campus
- Internships in different area
- Off-campus job
- Weekend exploring

## Housing Tips

### Finding Housing
- **University housing:** Guaranteed freshman year usually
- **Off-campus:** Facebook groups, Craigslist, Apartments.com
- **Subletting:** Summer months, study abroad students
- **Roommate matching:** School services, apps

### Red Flags
- Too cheap for area → scam
- Wire money before seeing → scam
- Can't tour in person → likely scam

### Lease Tips
- 12-month standard, ask for academic year
- Get everything in writing
- Document condition at move-in
- Understand subletting rules
